Introduction to Roguelikes

What is a roguelike? First of all, it is my favorite genre of game! But on a serious note,
this is a question that has puzzled enjoyers of the genre for years,
as the definition gets applied to everything from the original Rogue to Fortnite.

Classic Roguelikes

Developers at the International Roguelike Development Conference of 2008
argued for many hours about what defines a Roguelike, eventually deciding
on 13 key factors, known as the Berlin Interpretation:

  1. The world of the game is randomly generated
  2. Death is permanent; you must create a new character after you die if you want to play again
